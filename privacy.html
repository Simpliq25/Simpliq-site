Privacy Policy

At Simpliq, we value your privacy and we have zero interest in selling your data to telemarketers or shady third parties. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. The Information We Collect
We only collect what is absolutely necessary to run this huddle: Contact Info (Your name and email address) and Payment Info (Your credit card details are handled entirely by Stripe; we never see your full card number and we don't store it on our servers).

2. How We Use Your Data
We use your information for two things and two things only: To send you the newsletters you paid for, and to manage your subscription (billing, renewals, and "Mutual Breakups").

3. Who We Share It With
We don't "sell" your data. We only share it with the tools required to actually run Simpliq: Stripe (to process your payments securely) and Email (to handle our communications).

4. Your Rights
Whether you are in California (CCPA), the EU (GDPR), or anywhere else, you own your data. You have the right to see it, fix it, or delete it.

5. Cookies & Security
Our website uses basic cookies to make sure the "Join" button works. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Since we use Stripe and secure email, your sensitive data is tucked away in some of the best digital vaults in the world.
