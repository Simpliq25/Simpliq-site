Simpliq

At Simpliq, we value your privacy and we have zero interest in selling your data to telemarketers or shady third parties. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. The Information We Collect

We only collect what is absolutely necessary to run this huddle:

Contact Info: Your name and email address.
Payment Info: Your credit card details are handled entirely by Stripe. We never see your full card number and we don't store it on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for two things and two things only:

  • To send you the newsletters you paid for.
  • To manage your subscription (billing, renewals, and "Mutual Breakups").

3. Who We Share It With (The Short List)

We don't "sell" your data. We only share it with the tools required to actually run Simpliq:

  • Stripe: To process your payments securely.
  • Email: To handle our communications.

4. Your Rights (The Legal Stuff)

Whether you are in California (CCPA), the EU (GDPR), or anywhere else, we believe you own your data. You have the right to:

  • See it: Ask us what info we have on you.
  • Fix it: Update your email or name.
  • Delete it: Request that we wipe your data from our system.
  • Stop it: Every email has an "Unsubscribe" link. Use it, and we’ll stop bothering you immediately.

5. Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to make sure the "Join" button works and you stay logged in. We aren't following you around the internet like a digital stalker.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Since we use Proton and Stripe, your most sensitive data is tucked away behind some of the best digital vaults in the world.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we change how we handle your data, we’ll update this page and let you know in a Tuesday Drop. We won't sneak anything past you.
